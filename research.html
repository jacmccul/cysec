SkyShield: Game-Theoretic Defense for Autonomous Drones

Conducting ongoing research on applying game-theoretic approaches and solutions to enhance the cybersecurity of unmanned aerial systems (UAS's). This work focuses on developing mathematically optimal defense mechanisms against evolving cyber threats, with for plans of future publication!
This research has been recently presented to the president of IU, Pamela Whitten, along with other individuals that are members of congress (both representatives and legislators).
Once research is published, a full paper will be posted here!
